# Concept Sheet · L1 — The Construction Hall

> SITE 009 · Stage 2 concept art document · derived from
> [`docs/bible/04-ROOMS/L1-TIMELINE.md`](../bible/04-ROOMS/L1-TIMELINE.md) — no redesign, only fixation.
> Station: TIMELINE (frontal elevation with four hard stops, one per stage). Tone scale 0–10.

## Composition

The one frontal hall: camera square to the building's long face,
elevation ≈ 13 degrees — the rostrum shot across drawings. Four plinths
in a strict row on 1.8 m centers, each 1.2 × 1.2 m, carrying the
degree-building at one construction stage; a single hairline ties all
four at datum height. Each *stop* frames one plinth centered with its
neighbors' edges breathing at the frame's sides — the sequence read one
sheet at a time. The eye at each stop enters at the session datum above
the massing, drops through the stamped blocks, and lands on the exempt
base half-buried at the plinth.

## Architecture

Each stage massing is built of the axono blocks at 1:160 of their sheet
coordinates: blocks ≈ 0.35 m cubes with drawn labels, three to a lift,
lift height 0.28 m. Stage I carries the exempt base plus the 2024 lift;
stages II and III add the 2025 lift (III adds the spring artifact
plate); stage IV tops out with the 2026 lift, the industry plate, and,
hung above it, the completion plate — resin, 0.9 × 0.3 m, ruled like a
certificate. The exempt base on every plinth: concrete a shade darker
than all above, half-sunk (0.14 m proud), edges drawn, faces unstamped.
Artifact plates: aluminum, 0.22 × 0.06 m, standing proud of the massing
flank like nameplates.

## Light

The key rakes along the row, so each stage throws its shadow toward the
next stop — and each later stage throws a *longer* one: time recorded
twice, in mass and in cast. Shadows tone 8, doubled; exempt bases sit
in the fill only, tone 6; completion plate the brightest small surface
(9.3), lit by nothing but position.

## Mood

Time as poured work. Earned by sameness (one object aging, not four
objects), the lengthening shadows, and the buried, unstamped base —
prior knowledge as found foundation.

## Interaction States — Three Plates

**Plate 1 · Stop I, resting.** Stage I centered; datum `2024 SPR` ruled
above; all graphite.

**Plate 2 · Stop IV, hover.** Stage IV's boundary heavy signal; block
stamps legible (`SOFT DEV STUDIO · 95 HD` at label size); `STEVTECH`
plate underlined as a door; completion plate reading `UTS BIT ·
COMPLETE · GPA 6.00/7.0 · WAM 80.31 · 144 CP`.

**Plate 3 · The pan.** Mid-travel between stops II and III: both stages
partially in frame, the datum line continuous, motion implied by
composition alone — the plate fixes that a stop-to-stop hop is 1.1 s on
the single ease with hard arrival, never free scroll.
