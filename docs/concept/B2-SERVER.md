# Concept Sheet · B2 — The Server Room

> SITE 009 · Stage 2 concept art document · derived from
> [`docs/bible/04-ROOMS/B2-SERVER.md`](../bible/04-ROOMS/B2-SERVER.md) — no redesign, only fixation.
> Station: SERVER (the deepest station, below the core). Tone scale 0–10.

## Composition

Lower and dimmer than B1: the camera below grade, oblique, the cut's
borrowed light arriving twice-diminished as one pale rectangle on the
chamber floor. One short aisle: four cabinets in a row along the back,
the second from left stenciled and answering; on the facing wall, the
bare patch — a rectangle faintly lighter than the wall, no caption. The
eye adjusts, finds the stencil, and the room is read.

## Architecture

Chamber floor 6 × 5 m at −2.2, tone 4 (the darkest ground in the
building); back wall tone 3.5. Cabinets: aluminum, 0.5 × 1.1 × 0.35 m
on 1.0 m centers, doors closed, flat elevations. The named cabinet
carries `SYS · SALIERI009` at reading height and the address line
`GITHUB.COM/SALIERI009` beneath. The bare patch: 1.2 × 0.7 m, wall
tone + 0.8, edges soft, mounted at heatmap height on the side wall.

## Light / Mood

One wash (tone 6.5 at its center) under the opening; cabinets catch it
on their top edges only. Cold stillness — what this room stores is not
running; it is kept.

## Interaction States — Three Plates

**Plate 1 · Resting.** All graphite/aluminum in the dim; the patch
legible only to a looking eye; no blue, no lights, no hum.

**Plate 2 · Hover — the door.** The named cabinet's outline at heavy
signal — the deepest blue in the building — leader note `ROOM · B2 ·
SYS · GITHUB`. Nothing else wakes.

**Plate 3 · The patch.** Tight crop: the lighter rectangle, uncaptioned.
The plate exists so the painter does not "fix" it.

## References

Data-hall elevations drawn flat; the removed-picture ghost on an old
wall; bank-vault stillness.
