# Concept Sheet · B1 — The Mechanical Core

> SITE 009 · Stage 2 concept art document · derived from
> [`docs/bible/04-ROOMS/B1-CORE.md`](../bible/04-ROOMS/B1-CORE.md) — no redesign, only fixation.
> Station: CORE (low oblique, below the slab line, inside the opened poché). Tone scale 0–10.

## Composition

The camera sits below grade, oblique, looking into the opened ground:
the paper sheet cuts away in a rectangular opening whose edges carry a
45-degree hatch band — sectioned solid, the drawing convention made
literal. Light leans in through the cut from above, brightest at the
opening (tone 8 wash) and falling to legible dim (tone 4.5) at the
chamber's edges. Five risers rise through the frame on meter centers,
piercing sleeved penetrations in the slab overhead; the thinner
intercom run crosses the back wall. The eye enters at the cut's bright
edge, follows the nearest riser down to its thimble, reads the gauge
plates left to right, and ends on the intercom line exiting toward the
library above.

## Architecture

Chamber: concrete floor 6 × 5 m at −2.2 m; raw concrete walls implied
by darkness beyond tone 4. Risers a–e: aluminum, 120 mm square, from
floor thimbles (concrete, 250 mm square, 150 mm tall) to slab sleeves
(aluminum collars 200 mm square at y ≈ 0). Gauge plates at reading
height (−1.1 m): resin, 0.3 × 0.18 m, stamped with trade letter, tool
stencil, proof mark, and destination line. Intercom: 50 mm run along
the rear wall at −1.4 m with its own smaller plate. The C gauge
(`DEEP LEARNING · 68 C`) is stamped at the identical size as the HDs —
the room's credibility is priced in that dial.

## Light

Borrowed only: the one sky through the cut. A pale rectangle of wash on
the chamber floor under the opening (tone 7.5); riser tops brightest
where they approach the slab (7); thimbles and floor corners at 4.5–5.
No other sources; nothing hums visibly. Shadows are the darkness
itself — this is the only room whose ground tone is below 6.

## Mood

Respect for the unseen — the plant room kept as carefully as the
facade. Earned by stencil discipline, the equal-size C, and the rule
that every pipe visibly *goes somewhere*: five penetrations, five
destinations, no orphan runs.

## Interaction States — Three Plates

**Plate 1 · Resting.** Metal and stencil in the dim; hatch band crisp
at the cut; no blue.

**Plate 2 · Hover — riser b.** The AI riser takes the current: heavy
signal running thimble → gauge → sleeve and *through*, exiting upward;
its gauge plate reads `DEEP LEARNING · 68 C · SAGEMAKER`, destination
`SERVES · A-101 CROWD`. The other four risers rest graphite.

**Plate 3 · The intercom.** Tight crop of the rear wall: the thin run,
its plate — `KO NATIVE · EN FLUENT · JA / DE LEARNING`, commission
`ROK ARMY · INTERPRETER`, destination `SERVES · L4 LIBRARY` — the
building's languages exhibited as infrastructure.
